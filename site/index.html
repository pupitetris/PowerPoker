Place the cards on the 5x5 board and make as many points as you can from the resulting Poker hands, both top-down and across. That's it!

This game is inspired on COMPUTE!'s Gazette 1985/11 (Issue 29) Power Poker game, coded in C64 Basic. This implementation is Copyright © 2008-2025, Arturo Espinosa Aldama and is released under the GNU GPL v2.
